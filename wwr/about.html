Wild Waters Rafting

At Wild Waters Rafting, our passion is the thrill of adventure on the wild waters. We provide unforgettable rafting experiences for all skill levels, navigating the heart-pounding rapids and breathtaking scenery of the African savanna. Our vsion is to make your next visit to the mighty Zambezi a memory that will last an eternity! Witness the true smoke that thunders and follow the rainbow that never disappears along the great Mosiatunya!

History

Wild Waters Rafting was founded in 1998 by a group of passionate adventurers who sought to share their love of white water rafting with others. We began with small, personalized expeditions on the Zambezi River, and over the years, we've grown into a leading provider of rafting adventures in Victoria Falls.
